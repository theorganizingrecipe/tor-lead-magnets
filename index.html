✦ The Checklist That Changes Everything ✦

After organizing 50+ homes in Denver, I've noticed the same pattern: every home holds things that are silently stealing space, energy, and peace. This is the list. Check off what resonates. Be honest with yourself.

"Clutter is not just physical stuff. It's old ideas, toxic relationships, and bad habits. Clutter is anything that does not support your better self."

— Lorena Duarte, Founder of The Organizing Recipe

What To Do With This List

01

Use it on your phone or print it — go room by room at your own pace.

02

Check every item honestly. No guilt. This is just information.

03

Start a donation box. Fill it with everything you checked. Watch it grow.

04

Notice how you feel when the space opens up. That feeling is the goal.

05

If it feels overwhelming — that's completely normal. That's what I'm here for.
